TSAR BOMBA

ELEMENTS REQUIRED

  • Comet Crash - Space
  • Amaurotic Lambent - light
  • Spiky Shield - Nature
  • Shoot Great Fire Blast at stunned enemy/li>

Imagine casually walking around and you see a giant flaming ball crash and blind you. The next thing you know, you've been disintegrated. This combo is basically that, an atomic bomb. Once your enemy is blinded from your comet crash, they will wait for their vision to come back and the next they see is their character, absolutly evaporated. This combo is very easy to use and doesn't require much aim, as all of your attacks are AOE.

COMBO STEPS

  1. Blind enemy with Comet Crash
  2. stun enemy with Amaurotic Lambent
  3. Activate Spiky Shield
  4. Aim Great Fire Blast at stunned enemy
