PLAYING WITH FIRE

ELEMENTS REQUIRED

  • Splitting Slime - Slime
  • Great Fire Blast - Fire
  • Scorching Basalt - Lava
  • Temporal Trap - Time

Fire and lava attacks are pretty cool, they can leave a burning AOE effect on the ground but nothing's stopping the enemy from simply just walking out of the AOE. So, why don't we trap them in place and force them to sit in the flames? This combo does exactly that, resulting in a slow, satisfying death while you just sit back and relax

COMBO STEPS

  1. Stun Enemy with Spitting Slime
  2. Aim Great fire Blast at stunned enemy
  3. Aim scorching basalt at stunned enemy
  4. Trap enemy in Temporal trap
  5. Watch as enemy burns in the temporal Trap

RAINING CHAOS

ELEMENTS REQUIRED

  • Bloodcurdling Blast - Nightmare
  • Space-Time Rupture - Chaos
  • Temporal Trap - Time

This combo will leave your enemy in fear as they look up and see chaos raining from above. Once your enemy gets stuck in your temporal trap, all they can do is helplessly watch as your Space-Time Rupture slowly melts them away for an instant kill. This combo is satisfying to pull off and is perfect if you're the type of player who likes to "savor" their kill.

COMBO STEPS

  1. Stun Enemy with Bloodcurdling Blast
  2. Activate Space-Time Rupture
  3. Trap stunned enemy in Temporal Trap
  4. Aim Space-Time Rupture at Trapped Enemy
